WHY IT'S DIFFERENT

Google Lens gives up.
ArtPeriod digs in.

Generic image search works on famous paintings. It fails completely on the stuff you actually find — unsigned regional oils, old prints, minor listed artists, estate-sale mysteries. That's exactly what we built this for.

🎯

Deep identification

Reads signatures, monograms, labels, and stamps. Cross-references museum collections, artist databases, and the Getty's authority files — not just a reverse image lookup.

📊

Real auction comps

Searches live auction records and sold prices to ground the value in what pieces like yours actually sell for — not a made-up number.

🛡️

Stolen-art check

Cross-references the FBI and INTERPOL stolen-art registries so you don't unknowingly buy or sell a flagged piece.

🏛️

Authentication routing

Tells you exactly who to contact to verify a piece — the right specialists, appraisers, and authentication boards for that artist or era.

📍

Where to sell

Matched to your piece: the right auction house, online platform, or local dealer — with the reasoning for each.

✍️

Instant listings

Generates ready-to-paste sale posts for eBay, Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, and Instagram — with a suggested price.
